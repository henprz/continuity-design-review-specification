# Continuity Design Framework

> A conceptual framework for evaluating how digital products support continuity throughout the user's journey.

The Continuity Design Framework defines the theoretical foundation of the Continuity Design project.

It introduces the concepts, principles, and evaluation dimensions used to understand how products participate in users' broader narratives beyond isolated interactions.

The framework is implementation-independent and serves as the conceptual basis for the Continuity Design Review Specification (CDRS).

---

# Purpose

The framework exists to answer a fundamental question:

> **How does a digital product contribute to the continuity of a user's journey?**

Rather than focusing exclusively on usability, efficiency, or interface quality, Continuity Design examines how products preserve context, support meaningful progress, and expand future possibilities.

---

# Relationship to CDRS

The framework defines:

- concepts;
- principles;
- evaluation dimensions.

The CDRS defines:

- how those concepts are evaluated;
- how reviews are conducted;
- how findings are reported.

The framework explains **what** Continuity Design is.

CDRS explains **how** it is applied.
