# Templates

This directory contains reusable templates for applying the Continuity Design Review Specification (CDRS).

Templates are informative artifacts that simplify the adoption of the specification. They are not part of the normative specification and may be adapted to different workflows.

---

## Review Templates

Review templates guide information collection before the evaluation begins.

Available templates:

- Quick Review
- Guided Review
- Comprehensive Review

---

## Report Templates

Report templates provide a standardized structure for communicating review results.

They follow the report structure defined by the CDRS while allowing flexibility in presentation.

---

## Relationship to the Specification

The CDRS defines **what information a compliant review must contain**.

Templates provide one possible way of organizing that information.

Different implementations may use different templates while remaining compliant with the specification.
