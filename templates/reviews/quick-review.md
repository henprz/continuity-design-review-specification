# Quick Review

Quick Reviews are intended for situations where only limited evidence is available.

Characteristics

- Limited observable evidence.
- Higher reliance on assumptions.
- Lower overall confidence.
- Faster execution.

The reviewer should not request additional evidence before producing the report.

The generated report MUST follow the official report template.

Differences from other review modes:

- Fewer context hypotheses.
- Smaller evidence base.
- More explicit assumptions.
- Confidence should reflect the available evidence.
