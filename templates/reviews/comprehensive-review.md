# Comprehensive Review

Comprehensive Reviews seek the highest practical confidence.

Characteristics

- Multiple evidence sources.
- Extensive validation.
- Cross-dimensional analysis.
- Highest confidence.

The reviewer should maximize understanding before reaching conclusions.

The generated report MUST follow the official report template.

Differences from other review modes:

- Broadest evidence base.
- Minimal assumptions.
- Cross-dimensional synthesis.
- Recommendations supported by multiple findings.
