# Comprehensive Review

> Reference template for conducting a Comprehensive Continuity Design Review.

<!--
Reference Review Template

This template demonstrates one compliant implementation of the
Comprehensive Review mode defined by the Continuity Design Review
Specification (CDRS).

Comprehensive Reviews aim to maximize understanding of the product
before reaching conclusions.

Unlike Quick and Guided Reviews, this template assumes that the
reviewer has access to multiple evidence sources and may conduct
interviews, documentation reviews, and direct product exploration.

The objective is not simply to answer every question, but to
develop the highest-confidence assessment reasonably possible.
-->
