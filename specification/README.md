# Continuity Design Review Specification (CDRS)

> A standardized method for applying the Continuity Design framework to digital product evaluation.

The **Continuity Design Review Specification (CDRS)** defines a structured, implementation-independent method for evaluating digital products through the principles of the **Continuity Design Framework**.

The specification standardizes how reviews are conducted, how evidence is analyzed, how findings are reported, and how confidence is communicated. It is designed to produce consistent, transparent, and actionable evaluations that can be performed by individuals, teams, software applications, or AI systems.

---

# Purpose

CDRS defines **how** the Continuity Design framework is applied during a product review.

The specification establishes:

- a standardized review method;
- supported review modes;
- accepted evidence sources;
- a confidence model;
- evaluation requirements;
- a standardized report structure; and
- conformance requirements.

The specification defines **what** a compliant review must accomplish without prescribing **how** it must be implemented.

---

# Relationship to the Framework

The Continuity Design Framework defines:

- the conceptual principles;
- the evaluation dimensions;
- the theoretical foundation.

CDRS defines:

- how those dimensions are reviewed;
- how findings are documented;
- how recommendations are produced;
- how review results are communicated.

The framework and the specification are complementary and evolve independently.

---

# Document Structure

The specification is organized into four parts.

## Part I — Foundation

Introduces the purpose, scope, terminology, and design goals of the specification.

## Part II — Review Method

Defines the review process, review modes, accepted inputs, and confidence model.

## Part III — Review Output

Defines how evaluation results are structured and reported.

## Part IV — Specification

Defines conformance, references, versioning, and informative appendices.

---

# Design Principles

CDRS was designed to be:

- repeatable;
- implementation-independent;
- transparent;
- evidence-based;
- AI-compatible;
- extensible.
